How to Make Indian Dishes

  • Hyderabadi Biryani

    Ingredients: Basmati rice, meat (chicken or mutton), yogurt, onions, spices (bay leaf, cardamom, cloves, cinnamon), saffron, and mint.

    Instructions:

    1. Wash and soak the basmati rice for 30 minutes.
    2. Marinate the meat with yogurt and spices for at least 1 hour.
    3. Fry onions until golden brown and set aside.
    4. In a pot, layer marinated meat, fried onions, soaked rice, and saffron mixed in milk.
    5. Cover tightly and cook on low heat for 45 minutes until the rice is fluffy and meat is tender.
  • Butter Chicken

    Ingredients: Chicken, butter, cream, tomatoes, garlic, ginger, and spices (garam masala, chili powder, cumin).

    Instructions:

    1. Marinate the chicken in yogurt and spices for 1 hour.
    2. In a pan, heat butter and sauté garlic and ginger.
    3. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until soft.
    4. Add marinated chicken and cook until done.
    5. Stir in cream and simmer for 10 minutes. Serve hot with naan.
  • Masala Dosa

    Ingredients: Rice, urad dal, potatoes, mustard seeds, curry leaves, and spices.

    Instructions:

    1. Soak rice and urad dal overnight, then grind to a smooth batter.
    2. Ferment the batter for 8 hours.
    3. Cook boiled potatoes with mustard seeds and curry leaves for the filling.
    4. Heat a griddle, pour batter, and cook until crispy.
    5. Fill with potato mixture and serve with chutney.
  • Chole Bhature

    Ingredients: Chickpeas, flour, yogurt, spices (cumin, coriander, garam masala), and onions.

    Instructions:

    1. Soak chickpeas overnight and cook until soft.
    2. Prepare bhature dough with flour, yogurt, and water; let it rest.
    3. Sauté onions and spices, then add chickpeas and cook.
    4. Roll out bhature and deep fry until golden.
    5. Serve chole with bhature and pickles.
  • Indian Thali

    Ingredients: Variety of dishes (dal, curries, rice, roti, pickles, and dessert).

    Instructions:

    1. Prepare individual dishes such as dal, vegetable curry, and rice.
    2. Serve with freshly made roti and pickles.
    3. Add a sweet dish for dessert.
    4. Arrange all items on a plate and enjoy!
  • Pani Puri (Golgappa)

    Ingredients: Semolina, chickpeas, potatoes, tamarind, and spices.

    Instructions:

    1. Prepare puris by mixing semolina and water, then fry until crispy.
    2. Cook chickpeas and potatoes, mash, and mix with spices.
    3. Make the tamarind water and season it with spices.
    4. Fill puris with the potato mixture and dip in tamarind water before serving.
